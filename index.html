In the Trevor Noah book, Trevor Noah describes how his mother, Patricia Noah, has influenced and given him strength.
He mentioned this in his book by saying, ‘I grew up in a world where authority was female.’ She taught him how to
face and overcome challenges. She did the impossible to raise him. And at the end of the day, she made him the way
he is today, a successful man in the world. So to explain both the mother and child, I would have given the title Trevor
Noah and Patricia Noah for the book Born in Crime. I believe women should be given admiration for what they have done.

Women in relationship and work

Today, no profession is meant to be only for a man or a woman. A Girl can do any job, even though some
work is not advisable, as it can cause damage to her physical body. To illustrate, I want to mention some of the women
who are known for their hard work.

Women in the past were not allowed to work, possess any property, or learn. The only thing they
could do was get married and give birth. This has damaged the psychology and health of women:
it make them think they cannot do anything other than raise children. Such an attitude affected
the family, country, and the world, because they have much to contribute and solve problems. So
I believe the whole community should be supportive and do whatever it takes for
women to get an education and access to health service.

Men are also part of the community, so they have to help encourage women. But from my experience,
most of them including Abel want a girl just to be in her home, and take care of the family. They do not have
a desire for their girl to be independent: they do not choose a free, confident girl who has the
potential to do all by herself. They want to get married to a girl who can make time for her family.
